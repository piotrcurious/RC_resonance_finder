- The series RC circuit consists of two RC subcircuits connected to a digital pin of the Arduino.
- First RC: R1 = 1 ohm (known), C1 = 0.5 uF (known).
- Second RC: R2 = Unknown, C2 = 10 uF (known).
- Internal resistance of digital pin R0 = 250 ohms (5V / 0.02A max current).
- Measurement pin: A0 measures voltage across C2.
- The system is a 2nd-order RC ladder.
- It does not have a resonant frequency in the standard sense (gain peak > 1).
- The strategy will be to find the frequency where the output voltage V_C2 has a specific gain or phase, and use that to calculate R2.
- A Kalman filter will be used to track R2 and its uncertainty over time.
- The process runs every 60 seconds.
- Frequency sweep or binary search can be used to find the frequency that satisfies a specific condition (e.g., gain = 0.5).
